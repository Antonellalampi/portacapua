ABOUT US

 We're the students of the Liceo Scientifico Federico Quercia in Marcianise (we're the classes 3G and 3I). We're here for the "alternanza scuola-lavoro" project.

ALTERNANZA SCUOLA-LAVORO PROJECT

The "alternanza scuola-lavoro" is an innovative teaching methodology estsablished by the law n.53/2003 and disciplined by the legislative decretee n.17/2005. This project is direct to the students of second-grade schools, to whom allows of alternate class hours with moments of in-company training. It gives to young people, in addiction to a basic knowledge, also skills to join the working world.
